The Current Storyline
As the Age of Despair waned, so did the Vampires, until they finally disappeared from even the shadows of Elveron. Races wove in and out of power throughout each epoch, yet only Vampires had matched the Humans in preserving their power since the original annals. Indeed, the two may have diverged from the same tribe, and that opposing togetherness sparked the first fears in Humans that their empire may also fall one day. The chief Human rulers discussed in secret correspondence how they could revolutionize their forces before they met the fate of their Evil brethren, who had succumbed to an army even more undead than they were. Was it possible that a race more human than Humans could arise?
